E-commerce Redesign

Project Background

Hello There

A leading fashion e-commerce platform approached us to redesign their website, which was experiencing high bounce rates and low conversion. The goal was to create a modern, engaging shopping experience that drives sales.

The Problem

Analysis revealed several critical issues:

  • Cluttered product pages with poor information hierarchy
  • Confusing checkout process with 7+ steps
  • Slow loading times affecting mobile users
  • Inconsistent design language across pages
  • Limited product discovery features

Our Approach

Research & Discovery

We conducted:

  • Heuristic evaluation of the existing site
  • User interviews with 30 customers
  • Analytics analysis to identify pain points
  • A/B testing of key user flows

Design Strategy

Our redesign focused on three pillars:

  1. Streamlined Shopping Experience

    • Simplified navigation with mega menus
    • Enhanced product filtering and sorting
    • Quick view functionality for products
    • Wishlist and comparison features

  2. Optimized Conversion Funnel

    • Reduced checkout to 3 steps
    • Guest checkout option
    • Multiple payment methods
    • Clear shipping information upfront

  3. Visual Excellence

    • Modern, clean aesthetic
    • High-quality product imagery
    • Consistent design system
    • Micro-animations for engagement

Key Features

Smart Search

Implemented AI-powered search with:

  • Auto-suggestions
  • Visual search capability
  • Filters for size, color, price, brand
  • Search history and trending items

Personalization

  • Recommended products based on browsing history
  • Personalized homepage
  • Size recommendations
  • Style quizzes

Mobile-First Design

  • Optimized for touch interactions
  • Fast loading with lazy loading images
  • Native app-like experience
  • One-handed navigation friendly

Design System

Created a comprehensive design system including:

  • Color palette and typography
  • Component library (buttons, cards, forms)
  • Iconography
  • Spacing and grid system
  • Animation guidelines

Impact

The redesign delivered impressive results:

  • +85% conversion rate improvement
  • -45% bounce rate reduction
  • +120% mobile sales increase
  • 3.2s average page load time (down from 8.5s)
  • 4.7/5 customer satisfaction rating

Testimonial

“The new design has transformed our business. Not only do we see better numbers, but customers genuinely love the experience. It’s exactly what we needed.”

— Sarah Johnson, Head of Digital

  • Abstract for version control
